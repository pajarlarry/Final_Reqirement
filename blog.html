Understanding the Basics of Web Development

Date: May 22, 2025

Web development is an essential skill in today's digital world. It involves creating and maintaining websites, and it can be divided into two main categories: front-end and back-end development. Front-end development focuses on the visual aspects of a website, including layout, design, and user experience, while back-end development deals with the server-side, databases, and application logic.

In this blog post, I will share some resources and tips for beginners looking to dive into web development, including popular programming languages, frameworks, and online courses.
